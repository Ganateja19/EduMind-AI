# EduMind AI - Final Deployment Guide

I have upgraded the project to support your `github.io` demo link request with a **Smart Configuration** feature. You do **not** need to edit any code!

## Step 1: Deploy Backend (Render)
1.  Click the blue **Deploy to Render** button in your [README](https://github.com/Ganateja19/EduMind-AI).
2.  Wait for the deployment to finish on Render.
3.  **Copy the URL** Render gives you (e.g., `https://edumind-ai-xxxx.onrender.com`).

## Step 2: Enable Frontend (GitHub Pages)
1.  Go to your GitHub Repository: [https://github.com/Ganateja19/EduMind-AI](https://github.com/Ganateja19/EduMind-AI).
2.  Click **Settings** (top tbar) -> **Pages** (left sidebar).
3.  Under **Source**, select **Deploy from a branch**.
4.  Under **Branch**, select `master` and `/ (root)`.
5.  Click **Save**.
6.  Wait about 1 minute. Refresh the page until you see your site is live at:
    **[https://Ganateja19.github.io/EduMind-AI/](https://Ganateja19.github.io/EduMind-AI/)**

## Step 3: Connect Them (One-Time Setup)
1.  Open your new live link: [https://Ganateja19.github.io/EduMind-AI/](https://Ganateja19.github.io/EduMind-AI/).
2.  The site will automatically ask you for the **Render Backend URL** (from Step 1).
3.  Paste it in. The site will save it and reload.
4.  **Done!** You now have a working AI predictor on `github.io`.

*Note: If you ever need to change the URL, verify clicking the "⚙️ Config Backend" button at the bottom right.*
